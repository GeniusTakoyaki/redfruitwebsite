Welcome to RedFruit: AI

Redfruit Classifier, an AI-powered web application designed to accurately distinguish between red fruits like apples and tomatoes using deep learning. Our cutting-edge model leverages advanced neural networks to analyze images and classify them with high precision. Whether you're a researcher, a farmer, or simply curious about fruit identification, our platform provides a seamless and efficient way to differentiate between these visually similar fruits.
